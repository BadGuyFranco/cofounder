# HubSpot Connector Capabilities

What this connector can do for you.

## Contacts

- Create, update, and delete contacts
- Search contacts by email, name, or properties
- View contact history and timeline

## Companies

- Create, update, and delete companies
- Search companies
- Link contacts to companies

## Deals

- Create, update, and delete deals
- Move deals through pipeline stages
- View deal history

## Engagements

- Log notes on contacts/companies
- Create and manage tasks
- Log phone calls
- Log emails
- Log meetings

## Lists

- Create contact and company lists
- Add/remove contacts from lists
- View list membership

## Associations

- Link contacts to companies
- Link deals to contacts/companies
- Manage custom associations

## Marketing

- View form submissions
- Enroll contacts in workflows
- Manage communication subscriptions

## Other

- Manage tickets (support)
- Manage products and line items
- Create and manage quotes
- Upload and manage files

## Limitations

- Some features require paid HubSpot subscriptions (Sales Hub, Service Hub, Marketing Hub)
- 100 requests per 10 seconds burst limit
- 250,000 requests per day
