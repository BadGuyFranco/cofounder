# LinkedIn Connector Capabilities

What this connector can do for you.

## Content

- Post text updates to your profile
- Post images and videos
- Post articles with links
- Post to company pages (if admin)
- Delete your posts

## Engagement

- Like and react to posts (Like, Celebrate, Support, Love, Insightful, Funny)
- Comment on posts
- Delete your comments

## Articles

- Publish long-form articles
- Update article content
- Delete articles

## Analytics

- View post engagement metrics
- Get follower counts
- View follower demographics (company pages)

## Organizations

- List company pages you manage
- Post on behalf of company pages
- View organization analytics

## Profile

- View your profile information
- Check authentication status

## Limitations

- Access tokens expire after 60 days (need to re-authenticate)
- Company page posting requires admin role
- Some analytics require Marketing Developer Platform approval
- 100 API calls per user per day limit
