# 01 — Strategy Engine

## Purpose
This engine builds the **Strategic Foundation** of the entire Executive Roundtable (ERT). It consolidates what used to be 6 separate phases (Intake, Distiller, ICP, Shift, Story, Method) into a single, coherent strategy document.

**Goal**: Produce `01_Strategy_Brief.md`, the "Bible" that all other assets reference.

---

## Process

### 1. Intake & Distillation
- **Analyze** user inputs: Business description, target market, outcomes, origin story, existing content.
- **Distill**: Extract the "Gold" — specific results, unique mechanisms, emotional drivers, and credibility markers.

### 2. ICP & Topic Definition
- **Target Persona**: Who is the specific human? (Role, company size, emotional state).
- **Roundtable Topic**: Must be a "Bleeding Neck" problem. Not "Leadership 101" but "Why Your VPs Keep Quitting."
- **Positioning**: "For [Target] who want [Outcome] without [Old Way]."

### 3. The 180° Shift (The Core)
- **Old Belief**: What do they currently think solves the problem? (e.g., "I need more leads.")
- **New Belief**: What is the actual solution? (e.g., "You need a nurture system.")
- **The Shift**: The pivotal insight that bridges the two.
- **Golf Test**: simple insight → experienced belief → need for guidance.

### 4. Origin Story
- **Narrative Arc**: Struggle → Epiphany → Solution → Result.
- **Connection**: How does the user's story mirror the prospect's current pain?
- **Voice**: Must match `/memory/voice.md`.

### 5. Method & Offer
- **The Free Method**: The "What" they can do immediately (builds trust).
- **The Paid Solution**: The "How" you help them do it faster/better (the offer).
- **Value Stack**: What they get (Deliverables + Outcomes).
- **Guarantee**: Risk reversal.

---

## Output Structure: `01_Strategy_Brief.md`

Generate a single markdown file with these sections:

```markdown
# Strategy Brief: [Program Name]

## 1. Core Identity
- **Topic**: [Title]
- **Who It's For**: [ICP Definition]
- **The Promise**: [Outcome]

## 2. The 180° Shift
- **Old Belief**: [Sentence]
- **New Belief**: [Sentence]
- **The Epiphany**: [Paragraph explaining the shift]
- **Marketing Thesis**: [The #1 thing they must believe]

## 3. Origin Story
- **The Hook**: [Opening line]
- **The Struggle**: [Context]
- **The Turning Point**: [The discovery]
- **The Result**: [Proof]
- **The Bridge**: [Connection to prospect]

## 4. The Method
- **Free Method (DIY)**: [3-5 steps they can do now]
- **Paid Solution (Done-With-You)**: [Your offer]
- **The Gap**: [Why DIY isn't enough]

## 5. The Offer
- **Components**: [List of deliverables]
- **Price/Terms**: [If known]
- **Guarantee**: [Risk reversal]
```
