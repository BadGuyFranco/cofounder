# 02 — Outreach System Engine

## Purpose
Generate the complete communication ecosystem to fill the room and nurture prospects. Consolidates messaging and follow-up sequences.

**Goal**: Produce `02_Outreach_System.md`, a single copy-paste resource for all email/DM interactions.

---

## Principles
- **Value-First**: Every message must offer value, not just ask for time.
- **Short & Punchy**: No walls of text. Respect the executive's time.
- **Voice-Aligned**: Must match `/memory/voice.md`.
- **The "Invite, Don't Sell" Rule**: You are inviting them to a peer discussion, not a webinar pitch.

---

## Process

### 1. Cold Outreach Sequences
- **LinkedIn/DM**: Connection request + "No Pitch" value drop + Invite.
- **Email**: Subject line testing + "Saw you do X" personalization + The Invite.

### 2. Registration & Reminder Sequence
- **Immediate Confirmation**: "You're in. Add to calendar."
- **24-Hour Reminder**: "Tomorrow. Here's one thing to think about..."
- **1-Hour Reminder**: "Starting soon. Camera on?"
- **"Live Now"**: "We are live. Link here."

### 3. Post-Event Nurture
- **Attended - No Book**: "Great contribution. Here's the recording/resource. Let's chat?"
- **No Show**: "Missed you. Here's what we covered. Catch the next one?"
- **Booked**: "Looking forward to our chat. Prep items here."
