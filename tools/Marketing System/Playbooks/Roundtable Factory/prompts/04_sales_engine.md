# 04 — Sales Engine

## Purpose
Convert the appointments generated by the roundtable into closed deals. Consolidates the Sales Script and Proposal Template.

**Goal**: Produce `04_Sales_Conversion.md`.

---

## Process

### 1. The Strategy Session Script
- **Discovery**: Deep dive into the "Open Loop" gap from the roundtable.
- **Diagnosis**: Confirm they fit the ICP.
- **The Pitch**: Prescription of the "Paid Solution" (from Strategy Brief).
- **Objection Handling**: Scripts for common pushbacks (Time, Money, Authority).

### 2. The Proposal
- **One-Page Proposal**: Structure for a high-converting, simple agreement.
- **Options**: Tiered pricing (if applicable).

---

## Output Structure: `04_Sales_Conversion.md`

```markdown
# Sales Conversion Asset Kit

## Part 1: The Strategy Session Script
**Phase 1: Discovery**
- "On the roundtable, you mentioned X..."

**Phase 2: Diagnosis**
- "Here is the gap I see..."

**Phase 3: Prescription**
- "I recommend [Offer Name]..."

## Part 2: Proposal Template
[Markdown template for the proposal document]
```
