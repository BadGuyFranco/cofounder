# CoFounder -- Lessons

Corrections and patterns captured during build sessions. Write here immediately after founder corrections. Don't wait until session end.

## How This Works

1. **Capture:** When the founder corrects your approach, write a one-liner here immediately with a destination tag.
2. **Hot list:** Recent corrections live here and are scanned at session startup.
3. **Graduate:** When a lesson has been absorbed into a permanent standard (session instinct, cursor rule, development guide), mark it graduated and note the destination.
4. **Prune:** Every 5th session, check for graduated lessons and remove them from the hot list.

## Format

```
- [YYYY-MM-DD] [Lesson text] -> Destination: [where this should eventually live]
  - Graduated: [date] -> [file where it was absorbed]
```
