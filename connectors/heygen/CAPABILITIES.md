# HeyGen Connector Capabilities

What this connector can do for you.

## Video Generation

- Create AI avatar videos from text scripts
- Generate videos using your custom avatars
- Create videos from templates with variable substitution
- Check video generation status and progress
- Download completed videos
- List all your generated videos

## Avatars

- List all public/stock avatars available
- List your custom/trained avatars
- View avatar details (poses, outfits, expressions)
- Get avatar preview images

## Templates

- List available video templates
- Create videos from templates with custom variables
- Preview template structure and required inputs

## Voices

- List available text-to-speech voices
- Filter voices by language and gender
- Preview voice characteristics

## Video Translation

- Translate existing videos to different languages
- Lip-sync translation for natural appearance

## Photo Avatars

- Create videos from still photos
- Animate photo subjects with AI

## Limitations

- Video generation takes 1-5 minutes (not instant)
- Concurrent generations limited by plan
- Custom avatars require Business plan or higher
- Video translation requires Enterprise plan
- Credits consumed per video based on duration
- Generated videos stored for limited time (download promptly)
- Some voices/languages may have availability limits
